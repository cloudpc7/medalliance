Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation.

Our application does not permit content, behavior, or activities that
involve the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. This includes, but
is not limited to:
- Sexual content involving minors
- Grooming or solicitation of minors
- Trafficking or exploitation of children
- Any attempt to harm or exploit a minor

Any content or accounts found to violate this policy will be removed.
We will report violations to the appropriate authorities when required
by law and cooperate fully with law enforcement.

We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and complying with
all applicable child protection laws and Google Play policies.
